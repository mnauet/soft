------------------------------------
wlan default setting rename:


Edit your /etc/default/grub changing the line from

    GRUB_CMDLINE_LINUX=""

    to

    GRUB_CMDLINE_LINUX="net.ifnames=0 biosdevname=0"

    and, finally:

    $ sudo update-grub

    and reboot your system:

    $ sudo reboot
